Solar PV Systems

Solar PV systems blend electrical design with environmental constraints. Site evaluation (tilt, azimuth, shading) and correct string/inverter sizing are foundational. Data logging helps detect soiling, shading, and inverter faults early.

Performance evaluation relies on accurate irradiance and temperature measurement. Regular O&M, simple SOPs for commissioning, and a clear loss budget improve long-term yields.
