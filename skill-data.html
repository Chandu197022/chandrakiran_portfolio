Data Analytics

Data analytics transforms raw logs into actionable insights. For energy systems, common KPIs include Performance Ratio (PR), Yield, and Capacity Utilization Factor (CUF). Time-series handling is essential: resampling, gap-filling, and alignment across sensors are recurring tasks.

I use pandas for ETL tasks, matplotlib or simple JS charts for visualization, and small rule-based checks to flag anomalies. Good visualization makes faults visible quickly — trend plots and heatmaps are my go-to tools.

Automation is key: scheduled parsing, KPI generation, and summary reports reduce manual work. Combine domain knowledge (PV losses, irradiance behavior) with statistical checks to avoid false positives.
